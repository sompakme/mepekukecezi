Squid Game The Challenge Bee is a serious threat with a down-to-earth gameplan Fri 24 Nov

August 12, 2024
blog

Bee from Squid Game The Challenge has a very straightforward strategy during her time on the Netflix show – “Do what feels right at the time.” She’s a big believer that authenticity is key and struggles with the person-to-person eliminations.

The Netflix reality star describes herself as a “serious threat” among the other 455 players taking part. Her intelligence shines through during episode 3 and her gaming experience comes in handy, too. Bee has her eyes on the million-dollar prize fund and has hopes of buying herself a farmhouse with animals should she win.

Meet Bee from Squid Game The Challenge

Player 018, Bee, is ready to take on Squid Game: The Challenge.

She hails from London, UK, and is 28 years old.

Speaking during the show, the Netflix star explains that she “felt different” as a child.

At around nine years old, she said that she was feeling “isolated” and “more depressed than other people her age.”

By 13, Bee was in Mensa and had an IQ score in the top 0.5 per cent of people in the UK.

Speaking of the achievement, Bee explained that her parents encouraged her to take part in it so that she would “see that she is capable on paper.”

She’s a gaming pro

During Squid Game The Challenge episode 3, War, the contestants are tasked with a game of human Battleships, ‘Warships’.

Going into the game, Bee says confidently: “I’m really good at games. I play games for a living, if there’s anything I’m going to be good at, it’s this.”

The Squid Game star showed off her maths and logistic skills during the round. She adds in a confessional that she “takes a lot of pride in her intelligence and loves reading.”
