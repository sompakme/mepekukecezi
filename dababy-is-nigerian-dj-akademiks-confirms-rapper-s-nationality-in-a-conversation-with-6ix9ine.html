Popular American media personality, DJ Akademiks, has left many surprised after he recently came out to confirm the nationality of rapper, Jonathan Lyndale Kirk, who is mostly known as DaBaby.

According to the entertainer, the rap musician has some Nigerian blood in him.

Legit.ng sighted a video that captured the moment DJ Akademiks made the disclosure during a conversation with rapper 6ixnine.

The two appeared to have been talking about DaBaby’s music and in the middle of the conversation, 6ixnine noted that the fellow rapper dropped “an entirely Spanish record”.

Reacting, Akademiks was quick to interject and note that the rapper is Nigerian a little and as such, it’s better to say he dropped an African record.
