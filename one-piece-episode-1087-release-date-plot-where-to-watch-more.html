One Piece Episode 1087 Release Date, Plot, Where To Watch &amp; More

September 26, 2024
blog

The eagerly awaited “One Piece Episode 1087” is set to premiere on Sunday, December 10, 2023, at 9:30 am JST, promising to unravel new adventures and developments in the iconic anime series. With the conclusion of the Wano arc, fans are on the edge of their seats, anticipating the next chapter in the Straw Hats’ journey. This episode is expected to pivot the narrative towards new horizons, following the impactful events that have recently unfolded.

Post-Wano Developments

“One Piece Episode 1087” marks a significant transition from the intense Wano arc. The focus is likely to shift towards the aftermath and its implications on the larger world of the series. Viewers might get a glimpse into how the world outside Wano Country reacts to the recent upheavals and the direction in which the Straw Hats are headed next.

International Release and Where to watch

For international fans, the wait won’t be long. The episode will be available on Crunchyroll approximately 90 minutes after its Japanese release. This quick turnaround ensures that fans across various time zones can enjoy the episode almost concurrently with the Japanese audience. However, it’s important to note that Funimation, another streaming platform for the series, might have a slightly delayed release.

  • Eastern Standard Time: 9 pm, Saturday, December 9, 2023
  • Greenwich Mean Time: 2 am, Sunday, December 10, 2023
  • Central European Time: 3 am, Sunday, December 10, 2023
  • Indian Standard Time: 7:30 am, Sunday, December 10, 2023
  • Philippine Standard Time: 10 am, Sunday, December 10, 2023
  • Japanese Standard Time: 11 am, Sunday, December 10, 2023
  • Australia Central Standard Time: 12:30 pm, Sunday, December 10, 2023

Recap of Episode 1086

The preceding episode, “One Piece Episode 1086,” dealt with the aftermath of the Straw Hats’ departure from Wano and the humorous yet significant event of receiving new bounties. It also shed light on the Cross Guild, a new alliance formed by Mihawk, Crocodile, and Buggy, highlighting the dynamics within this newfound association.

Anticipated Plot of Episode 1087

While “One Piece Episode 1087” is expected to delve into the broader world events, it might also drop hints about the Straw Hats’ next destination. Manga enthusiasts speculate that Egghead Island could be the upcoming stop, though the anime’s pace in reaching this storyline remains to be seen.

As the “One Piece” saga continues to captivate its audience, Episode 1087 stands as a gateway to new adventures and unknown territories. With the conclusion of the Wano arc, the anime is poised to take its fans on another thrilling ride, keeping the spirit of exploration and adventure alive in the hearts of its viewers.
