Tankini swimsuits are one of our favorite bathing suit styles. These swimsuits have the coverage of one-piece swimwear with the comfortable fit and feel of a two-piece, and they’re also a great option for those who enjoy water and beach sports — think: surfing, paddleboarding, and beach volleyball — and want to protect their skin from the elements. 

We love tankini swimsuits because they’re also incredibly versatile in design and many offer additional benefits like built-in bra tops, tummy control, and shapewear technology. Who Should Wear a Tankini?

Tankinis are a bathing suit style that looks amazing on all body types. They’re also an excellent choice for those with longer torsos, as some styles can provide that one-piece look without tugging or digging into the body. Some tankini styles also have built-in shapewear technology that can sculpt and smooth the body for an added boost of confidence. 

We love tankini bathing suits for everyday beach and pool ensembles, but they’re also a great option for more athletic activities, including beach volleyball, kayaking, surfing, and paddleboarding, since they provide a little more coverage.
