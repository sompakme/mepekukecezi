Louis Vuittons monogrammed lipstick case is the luxury beauty accessory of the season

July 05, 2024
blog

Behold, an ultra-chic accessory for your accessory. Louis Vuitton has launched a new monogrammed lipstick case, first seen in its spring/summer 2019 collection, and yours for a cool £830.

Available in stores mid-March, the bullet case, which was inspired by 1920s jewellery holders, comes in two colourways: chocolate with gold hardware, and tan with silver.

Courtesy

Courtesy

Both are designed to be worn as a long statement necklace, cross body, or attached to your bag. We can picture the street style inspiration and flat lay showcases already.

They’re launching alongside Louis Vuitton's high-luxe monogram wireless Horizon earphones that mark the French house's first-ever foray into the audio market.

Another accessories first from the brand came last autumn/winter with the drop of its debut home fragrance collection, four perfumed candles presented in minimal white ceramic cases with gold lids.

So many ways to dress your life in Louis Vuitton...
