John Goodman’s Early Life

John Goodman was born on June 20, 1952, in Affton, Missouri. He developed an interest in acting during his high school years and pursued this passion at Southwest Missouri State University. After graduating, he moved to New York City to start his acting career.

Goodman’s breakthrough came in 1985 when he starred in the Broadway show “Big River”. His performance caught the attention of Hollywood producers, leading to roles in films like “Raising Arizona” and “The Big Easy”.
