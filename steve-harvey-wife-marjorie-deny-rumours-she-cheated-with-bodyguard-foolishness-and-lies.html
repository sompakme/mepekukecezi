US stand-up comedian and TV host Steve Harvey and his wife Marjorie have come out to shut down claims that their marriage was rocky.

This was after rife speculation went viral on social media about the two divorcing after Marjorie allegedly cheated on the V host with his bodyguard and chef.

Social media users had a blast condemning Steve for always praising his wife, who, according to them, was on her way out of their union.

The rumours also claimed that Marjorie had filed for divorce and demanded a huge chunk of the comedian's property.

But, Marjorie, via her Instagram page, shut down claims that there was trouble in paradise and instead told off haters with a bible verse.

"My husband and I don’t usually stop to address all the foolishness and lies that have been spread about us. However, to whom much is given much is required. I understand that with my platform comes some sort of responsibility to those that may not be as strong as we are," she wrote.

According to toofab.com, Steve also denied his marriage was in shambles. He said:

"Before I get started, just let me say, I’m fine, marriage is fine, still going strong, I don't know what y'all are doin', but find something else to do 'cause we fine, lord have mercy," Steve told the crowd, which immediately erupted in cheers.

Steve on breaking up with wife at his lowest

“I met her in the 1980s. I was performing in Memphis, Tennessee and she came to my show late. When I saw her I could not believe it. The audience started giggling because I was just staring at her,” he narrated.

According to him, he swore to marry her one day in 1988. However, they broke up not long afterwhen he became homeless.
