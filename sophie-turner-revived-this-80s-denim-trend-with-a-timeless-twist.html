Sophie Turner Revived This '80s Denim Trend With a Timeless Twist

September 02, 2024
blog

If trend forecasters can surmise anything from Sophie Turner’s wardrobe, it should be that a major ’80s denim trend is coming back in a big way.

Khaite The Danielle Acid-Wash Jeans

Khaite The Danielle Acid-Wash Jeans

The Game of Thrones alum is clearly a champion of the acid-wash revival, if her streak of recent street-style moments are any indication. Most recently, Turner stepped out in New York City wearing her beloved pair of high-rise acid-wash jeans from Khaite. She paired the statement pants with a plain black T-shirt from Bleusalt and a long black coat, a timeless fall staple.

For a preppy touch, the actor capped off the look with a pair of chunky black patent leather loafers by Billini. Accessories included a white Louis Vuitton purse with a black diamond pattern and a gold chain strap, plus black Ray-Ban sunglasses.

The Image Direct

Turner has been spotted out and about in her acid-wash Khaite jeans on multiple occasions.

For a more pared-down look from early October, she styled them with a light gray zip-up hoodie jacket, a jersey T-shirt, black shades, and red satin ballet flats from the Row.

Gotham//Getty Images

On yet another occasion, she wore the Khaite jeans with some more playful pieces, which included a white T-shirt emblazoned with a large yellow happy face from Re/Done. She tucked the tee into waistband of her pants, then layered with a navy blue zip-up sweater from Brandy Melville. At the time, she accessorized the look with black Ray-Ban shades, a white leather half-moon bag from the Row, and a pair of light blue and gray Nike sneakers.
