Introduction :

Ayla Woodruff, the good-looking blonde is an Internet star, known for her social media following, such as her Instagram account and YouTube channel.

Ayla Woodruff Family and Early Life :

  • She is the eldest of the couple’s four children & her siblings’ names are Blake Woodruff, Raina Woodruff and Trevor Woodruff.

College Education :

  • After high school, Ayla attended the University of California San Diego (UCSD) in the La Jolla neighborhood of San Diego, California.
  • Here, she accomplished to get a degree in Bachelor of Arts in Psychology with a focus in Industrial and Organizational Psychology.
  • While at the University, Ayla was a part of the Psychology club and was also a dog foster parent with the San Diego Humane Society.
  • She graduated college with the class of 2015.
  • After this, she pursued a certificate in Human Resource Management from Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, California. She completed this certification in December 2015.
