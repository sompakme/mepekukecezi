Recipes Unlocked

To unlock recipes with Spider Venom, you'll need to visit any Field Stations scattered across the yard. Once you're there, you'll be able to research the Spider Venom. But you'll need to be holding it in your inventory first. Listed below are all the recipes you'll unlock after scanning it.

  • Spider Fang Dagger

How to Get Spider Venom

Spider Venom can only be picked up once you kill either an Infected Wolf Spider or a Wolf Spider. Spiders are extremely dangerous to fight, and Wolf Spiders can deal incredible damage. You'll need to craft the strongest armor and weapons and train hard to use perfect blocks against their attacks.

Giant spiders come in two forms - the large yellow Orb Weaver Spiders and the even larger predators known as the Wolf Spider. Both are located in great numbers around the oak tree on the north side of the yard, as well as along the posts planted by the roses to the east and the large hedge area in the southeast.

Where to Find Spider Venom

  • BBQ Spill
  • Central Grasslands

  • Sandbox
  • Spider Caves
  • Undershed
  • Upper Grasslands
  • Woodpile
