FAQs

Can I get free shipping at Coach Outlet?

Yes. Coach Outlet has free shipping on all orders of $50 or more. You will need to be in continental USA for this to apply. If you sign up for the Coach Insider program, you will also get free shipping with no minimum spend. 

How can I get an extra 20% off discount at Coach Outlet?

Be sure to shop the sale section first for the latest discounts at Coach Outlet. Occasionally, you’ll be able to use extra discounts on sale items. For example, we’ve previously seen an extra 20% off bags and wallets when you buy two or more in the sale.  

Does Coach Outlet have a rewards program?

Yes. Sign up for the Coach Outlet rewards program—Coach Insider—and you can earn points on your purchases. You’ll also get early access to sales, personalized offers, and a birthday gift.  

When are the Coach Outlet sales?

Coach has sales regularly throughout the year on its website. We’d recommend shopping the key dates such as Black Friday and end-of-season sales. We’ve previously noted up to 70% off with Coach Outlet sales. If you miss the sales, check the dedicated ‘clearance’ section or pick up a Coach Outlet promo code. 

What is the return policy at Coach Outlet?

If you would like to return your Coach Outlet order, you can do so within 30 days of receipt. All items should be unworn and unused, with any tags or accessories still attached. Items excluded from returns include fragrances and leather care products.  

Hints and Tips

Coach Outlet Rewards: Sign up for the Coach Outlet rewards program—Coach Insider—to get the most out of your shop. You can sign up with your mobile number or preferred email address. Once you do, you’ll get early access to sales and products, free shipping, personalized offers, and a birthday gift each year. Most recently, you can get a Coach Outlet rewards code for $20 off your first order of $150 or over when you sign up. 

Refer a friend: Do you know someone who loves designer handbags? Refer your friend to Coach Outlet via the Coach Insider loyalty program. Your friend will get a $10 off Coach Outlet coupon for their first order with your referral link. Once they make their first purchase, you’ll also be sent a $10 off code for your next order.

Friends & Family sale: Shop the Friends & Family sales at Coach Outlet to save on designer accessories. Coach Outlet has an extra 15% off already discounted handbags and accessories. These sales happen semi-regularly and are highly sought-after so be sure to check here for the latest updates. 

Shop the clearance: If you’ve missed the seasonal sales, you can still save with the Coach Outlet clearance page. You can shop signature handbags, elegant totes, and iconic prints for less. Our savings experts have previously noted as much as 75% off selected items with the clearance page.
