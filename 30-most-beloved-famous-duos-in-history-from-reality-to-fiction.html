1. Romeo and Juliet (Romeo and Juliet)

Romeo, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, and Juliet, played by Claire Danes, are among the most famous duos in history. They are characters featured in William Shakespeare’s novel of the same title. Their story highlights intense love amid unfavourable circumstances. It is one of the most-told stories in modern literature.

3. Bonnie and Clyde (Bonnie and Clyde)

Bonnie and Clyde were romantic and criminal partners whose stories formed the basis of many movies and novels. Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson are among the famous duos in movies.
