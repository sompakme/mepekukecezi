Gigi Hadid Reveals Sneak Peek of Her Tommy Hilfiger Collection

September 17, 2024
blog

Back in December we learned that Gigi Hadid is collaborating with Tommy Hilfiger on a capsule collection, and now we have our first look at the upcoming line. The model, who has been busily shooting the campaign for the collection, took to her Snapchat to debut a few designs, and we're definitely liking what we see.

In the Snap, Hadid showcases a lineup of patches in red, white, and blue. A few of the patches are emblazoned with "H," which can stand for both Hadid and Hilfiger, while another design features her first name. While this tease doesn't give us much to go on, it's clear that the capsule will be all-American—just like her.

Look out for her collection with Tommy Hilfiger when it hits stores this fall.
