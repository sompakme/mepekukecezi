What Avengers Fans Want for Christmas

September 07, 2024
blog

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

It’s nearly impossible for even the most dedicated Avengers fan to collect every piece of Marvel memorabilia — but thankfully, the holidays are an opportunity to help your favorite pop culture devotee add to their collection. And this year has been a gift for Marvel Cinematic Universe superfans thanks to blockbusters such as The Marvels, Guardians of the Galaxy, Vol. 3, The Eternals, Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings and Black Widow, plus new spinoff series including The Falcon and the Winter Soldier, Hawkeye, Loki, WandaVision, Ms. Marvel, Moon Knight and others

Related Stories

The plethora of MCU gifting options means there’s enough franchise merchandise to fill another variant’s universe. That’s why we’ve rounded up more than 30 of the best Marvel gifts that fans of all ages will want to unwrap, from clever coffee mugs and stylish accessories to comfy pajamas and Advent calendars. Whether they’re a film buff, an accessories collector, a makeup maven, a sneakerhead or a pin trader, check out these top Marvel gift ideas for the holidays and beyond.

Need more gifting ideas? Check out The Hollywood Reporter‘s holiday shopping guides.

The Story of Marvel Studios Box Set

For the Marvel superfan who can’t get enough of every aspect of the MCU, they can soak up even more knowledge with every page of the Story of Marvel Studios box set. Co-authors Tara Bennett and Paul Terry were given “unprecedented access” to the producers, actors, writers, directors, costume designers, composers and many other creatives involved in dreaming up all 23 movies in The Infinity Saga, resulting in a 512-page chronicle of how Marvel made movie history. The two-volume set boasts over 200 interviews, a foreword by Marvel Studios president Kevin Feige and an afterword by Robert Downey Jr., plus over 500 production photos and never-before-seen images of memorabilia and archival materials.

Dr. Squatch Soap Avengers Collection

Give the gift of fresh scents! Dr. Squatch Soap Avengers Collection has four bars of natural men’s soap with everyone’s favorite Avengers, The Hulk’s Scrub Smash, Captain America’s Liberty Lather, Iron Man’s Stark Scrub and Thor’s Divine Storm.

MCU: The Reign of Marvel Studios

Written by pop culture writers Joanna Robinson, Dave Gonzales and Gavin Edwards, MCU: The Reign of Marvel Studios is an unauthorized behind-the-scenes look at one of Hollywood’s most dominant players. The book takes a full view of the studio and features more than 100 interviews with actors, producers, directors and writers who have all worked with Marvel Studios. This is ideal for any Marvel fan who wants an authentic looks at their favorite movies.

LEGO Avengers Tower

This LEGO Marvel set has more than 5,200 pieces and 31 mini-figures, so the Marvel fan in your life can spend hours upon hours putting together Avengers Tower and re-creating the Battle of New York from the day-after Christmas until New Year’s Day.

Iron Man Mark 1 Arc Reactor

If you’re a Pepper Potts looking for a gift for your Tony Stark, then the Iron Man Mark 1 Arc Reactor from the first Iron Man movie is ideal for the holidays — especially with the phrase “Proof That Tony Stark Has A Heart” printed on it.

The Avengers Mug by Heroes and Villains

From Heroes & Villains, this Avengers Mug has schematics of Thor, The Hulk, Captain America and Iron Man. It’s one of the best ways to wake up in the morning, especially with hot coffee inside.

Marvel Legends Socks (6-pack)

Although very few people like getting socks for the holidays, most Marvel fans do like getting socks as stocking stuffers — especially if they’re Marvel socks. This six-pack has colorful and white socks for Captain American, The Hulk and Iron Man.

Marvel Avengers Slippers

What’s more cozy than wearing a pair of Marvel Comics slippers? These Marvel slip-on house shoes have comic book panels from the ’60s, which gives them a vintage look.

Marvel’s Black Panther The Official Wakanda Cookbook

With recipes by Chef Nyanyika Banda, the Marvel’s Black Panther The Official Wakanda Cookbook has more than 70 recipes and meal ideas that are based on African tradition with a Marvel Studio’s twist, such as Roasted Chambo, Braised Oxtail and Dumplings, Glazed Road Runner Wings and more.

Marvel Deadpool Riding A Unicorn On A Rainbow T-Shirt

Only Deadpool could make riding a unicorn on top of a rainbow look so badass and cool. This Deadpool tee comes in black and features everyone’s favorite “Merc with the Mouth” in action.

Hawkeye “Rogers” The Musical Poster T-Shirt

Your favorite Clint Barton fan won’t have to endure the excruciating pain of watching “Rogers” the (fictional) musical in order to get this official Hawkeye tee, which comes in women’s, men’s and kids sizes.

Marvel Cooking with Deadpool Cookbook by Marc Sumerak and Elena Craig

The Merc with a Mouth also has a palate for chimichangas, pancakes and pickle-free burgers — and if that describes the diet of the Deadpool devotee on your list, then the wise-cracking anti-hero’s cookbook will have earn a rightful spot on their kitchen shelves. The 144-page book contains over 60 recipes ranging from stabby meat sticks and classic tuna casserole to double-shot cupcakes and an entire chapter devoted to the superhero’s favorite chimis.

Lego Marvel Infinity Gauntlet Collectible Building Kit

For the Lego and Marvel collectors, kill two birds with one (rather, six) Infinity Stones with this 590-piece Infinity Gauntlet. The building kit creates Thanos’ iconic Uru metal glove (in the form of plastic bricks) with movable fingers, and measures 12.5 inches high by five inches wide by four inches deep when completed. Note that it’s designed to be displayed, not worn.

Evo IRM-1 Rechargeable Sonic Toothbrush

Hey Jarvis, we need a high-end smart dental device made in Tony Stark’s image. Ask and ye shall receive in the form of Evo’s IRM-1 rechargeable sonic toothbrush, the perfect gift for Iron Man fans who value oral hygiene and want their bathroom to reflect their fandom. It has four brushing modes (standard, deep clean, sensitive and whitening) and the handle can be submerged under a meter of water for up to 30 minutes (for when your giftee wants to brush in the bathtub or a pool). A two-minute smart timer with 30-second intervals keeps their cleaning session in order, and the built-in memory automatically starts up in the last used mode.

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 Launch Edition Video Game for PS5

For the PS5 gamers, let them develop their virtual spidey senses with Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 Launch Edition. Web-slinging players can gear up as Miles Morales or Peter Parker in an adventure to save new York City from the evil Kraven The Hunter.

Cufflinks, Inc. Marvel Comics Stripe Silk Tie

This 100 percent silk jacquard striped tie is a subtle way for recipients to channel MCU heroes (or serve as a conversation starter) at their next awkward office party and upcoming holiday soirées.

Eternals Jewelry Collection Gift Box

Though it won’t arrive in time for Christmas, this luxe jewelry collection inspired by the Eternals will be a welcome surprise for stylish superhero fans come January. The limited-edition cosplay-ready box set includes an adjustable Black Knight ring, Sersi’s azurite-adorned chain necklace, Thena’s tiara and a hinged Uni-mind bracelet, plus a certificate of authenticity for each of the 5,500 limited-edition sets made.

Funko Marvel Advent Calendar

What’s better than opening one cool Marvel gift? A Funko Advent calendar filled with 24 days of surprises featuring palm-sized superheroes, vehicles and other buildable brick sets. Now that we’re well into December, giftees can enjoy opening multiple surprises per day to catch up (and you’ll save a few bucks as the set is on sale).

Robe Factory Thor Hammer Tool Set

Aspiring Asgardians can swing a hammer just like Thor with this cool 44-piece tool set inspired by the Marvel hero. All of the household tools you need come in a Mjolnir-inspired case.

Corkicle Hulk Sport Canteen


Even if your recipient can’t bulk up in an instant like Bruce Banner, at least they’ll stay hydrated during workouts like their favorite Marvel hero with Corkcicle’s Hulk-inspired sport canteen. This 60-ounce reusable bottle keeps drinks hot for 12 hours and cold for 25.

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings Emblem Sweatshirt

For the chilly winter season ahead, gift your loved ones this cozy pullover sweatshirt inspired by the record-breaking Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings. The fleece unisex sweater is emblazoned with the character’s emblem using eco-friendly inks, and it has a ribbed crew neck, cuff and hems for comfort. (Want to impress stylish superfans of actor Simu Liu even more? Treat them to a luxe puffer from Canadian outerwear brand Nobis, for which the star is the newest global ambassador.)

Marvel Every Man Jack Collectors Box Body Wash Gift Set

Ever wondered what Captain America or Spider-Man smells like? Everyone can get scented and sudsy like a superhero with Every Man Jack’s Marvel-inspired body wash set, which makes a great stocking stuffer for the holidays. It includes Captain America’s lavender-meets-citrus Winter Mint, Iron Man’s bergamot-infused Crimson Oak, Black Panther’s clove-powered Wakanda Herbs and Spider-Man’s crisp and minty Fresh Air.

WandaVision S.W.O.R.D. Limited-Edition Pin

Fans of WandaVision and pin traders alike will love sporting their S.W.O.R.D. pride on their lapel with this limited-edition enamel pin. The official Marvel accessory measures two inches tall and boasts the Sentient Weapon Observation and Response Department’s logo.

Time Variance Authority Seal Two-Tone Coffee Mug

Even TVA agents (ahem, and variants) need their daily cup of joe, and this Loki-inspired coffee mug will help them drink up in the spirit of the Marvel universe. It’s available in 11- and 15-ounce sizes, and you can also get it as a travel mug or as a customizable “morphing” mug that reveals a hidden message or photo when it’s filled with a hot beverage.

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 x Warby Parker

Stylish specs-wearing men and women can sport their Spider-man pride on their fashionable frames, or swap the designs for various Peter Parker-style frames — thanks to Warby Parker. Pair’s optical and sun glasses are equipped with magnets that allow the wearers to mix and match the tops of their eyewear.

Loungefly x Marvel Iron Man Mini Backpack

This Loungefly mini-backpack comes with Iron Man’s iconic helmet with arc reactor that actually lights up. The faux leather bag measures nine inches wide by 12 inches tall by five inches deep, so it makes the perfect compact carryall for your recipient’s next trip to the Avengers campus.

Hallmark Mystery 2 Marvel Avengers Christmas Tree Ornament

Whether you’re looking for stocking stuffers for everyone in your Marvel-loving family or a quick and easy white elephant gift, these charming mystery Christmas ornaments are bound to surprise their recipient, who’ll unbox the Black Panther, Captain America, the Infinity Gauntlet, Iron Man, Groot, Loki or Spider-Man.

Iron Man Cuff Links

Spoiler alert: Tony Stark lives on with these stylish cuff links. They boast Iron Man’s legendary fire-engine red and gold helmet and they’re stamped with Marvel’s logo on the back.

Citizen Avengers Watch

Marvel lovers can keep track of time like a true Avenger with Citizen’s stainless steel watch, which features a Japanese quartz movement and a 45-millimeter case that showcases the time atop a vibrant blue and silver-tone face. The three-hand timepiece boasts the watchmaker’s Eco-Drive technology that’s powered by light, so recipients won’t need to worry about replacing the battery. The piece is finished with shatter-resistant crystal and a link bracelet, and it’s water-resistant up to 100 meters. The brand has other Marvel styles to choose from, including designs inspired by Stan Lee, Tony Stark, Captain America, Spider-Man, Black Widow, the Hulk and others.

Groove Life x Marvel Thor Icon Groove Belt

Wearers can harness the power of Thor (or at least use it to keep their pants up) with Grove Life’s durable belt inspired by the Asgardian hero. Available in 28- to 60-inch waist sizes, it’s made with durable webbing and an aluminum alloy buckle that snaps closed with neodymium magnets.

Funko Pop! Marvel The Winter Soldier Vinyl Figurine

For the giftee who somehow doesn’t have every single Funko figurine in their possession, help them complete their collection with this 3.75-inch vinyl figure of Bucky Barnes as The Winter Soldier.
