Who is Olivia Culpo Dating? A Look at the Models Love Life

July 15, 2024
blog

Olivia Culpo stands out as one of today’s most renowned and accomplished models globally. Her journey to fame began when she clinched the titles of Miss USA and Miss Universe in 2012. Since then, she has graced numerous magazine covers, made appearances in movies and TV shows, and successfully launched her own fashion and beauty ventures. However, beyond the limelight, questions arise about her personal life—her relationships, marital status, and current dating situation. Let’s delve into the details.

Olivia Culpo’s Engagement to Christian McCaffrey

Olivia Culpo is presently engaged to Christian McCaffrey, the NFL star known for his role as a running back with the Carolina Panthers. Their relationship was officially confirmed in January 2020, following initial rumors of romance in May 2019. The couple has since been inseparable, traveling the world, spending quality time with their families, and actively supporting each other’s careers. In a heartwarming Instagram post in April 2023, Culpo joyfully announced McCaffrey’s proposal, expressing her love and anticipation for a lifetime together.

Past Relationships: Olivia Culpo and Nick Jonas

Before her engagement to McCaffrey, Olivia Culpo was in a significant relationship with singer and actor Nick Jonas, now married to Priyanka Chopra. Their romance began in 2013 after meeting at the Miss USA pageant. Despite living together in Los Angeles and appearing as a perfect couple at red carpet events, they announced their breakup in June 2015 due to busy schedules and differing goals. Despite the challenges, they maintained a friendship.

Other Rumored Relationships

Aside from McCaffrey and Jonas, Olivia Culpo has been linked to various celebrities over the years, although these connections were either unconfirmed or short-lived.

  • Ryan Lochte: In September 2012, Olympic swimmer Ryan Lochte expressed admiration for Culpo, but she denied any romantic involvement, clarifying they were just friends.
  • Tim Tebow: Rumors circulated in October 2015 about Culpo dating former NFL player Tim Tebow. Allegedly, they parted ways in November 2015 due to differences in values, specifically Tebow’s commitment to remaining celibate until marriage.
  • Zedd: In May 2019, Culpo was seen with DJ and producer Zedd at Coachella, sparking dating speculation. However, no confirmation followed, and they appeared to be friends or acquaintances.

Conclusion

Despite occasional rumors and past relationships, Olivia Culpo remains a private individual regarding her personal life. With a focus on her career and ongoing projects in the fashion and entertainment industry, Culpo is currently engaged to Christian McCaffrey, and they radiate happiness and love. As a multifaceted star, we extend our best wishes to Olivia Culpo in both her personal and professional endeavors.
