Let's delve into the journey of this Belgian maestro.

Born on January 7, 1991, in La Louvière, Belgium, Eden Hazard's passion for soccer was evident from a young age. His early years in Belgium saw him honing his skills and making a mark in the under-21 squads. By 2008, he had joined the professional team, showcasing his prowess by scoring six goals in 45 appearances by May 2014.

Hazard's journey in the Premier League began with Lille, where he played from 2007 to 2012. During this period, he netted 50 goals in 192 appearances for the club. Individual Honors And Recognitions

Eden Hazard's talent didn't go unnoticed. He bagged the UNFP Ligue 1 Young Player of the Year award twice, in 2008-09 and 2009-10. He was also named the UNFP Ligue 1 Player of the Year for two consecutive seasons, 2010-11 and 2011-12. Furthermore, he made it to the UNFMP Ligue 1 Team of the Year for three consecutive seasons from 2009 to 2012. His accolades didn't stop there. In the 2013-14 season, he was honored with the PFA Young Player of the Year. Hazard was a part of the Premier League PFA Team of the Year in 2012-13, 2013-14, and 2014-2015.
