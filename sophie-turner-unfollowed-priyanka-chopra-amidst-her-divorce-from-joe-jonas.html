Sophie Turner Unfollowed Priyanka Chopra Amidst Her Divorce From Joe Jonas

September 02, 2024
blog

The Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas drama continues — and it seems that the J Sisters are sadly breaking up, too. According to E!, Turner no longer follows Joe's brother Nick's wife, Priyanka Chopra, despite previously being friends and sisters-in-law. Chopra also appears to have returned the favor and clicked the unfollow button, as well.

The outlet reports that as of Friday, Sophie is still following her estranged husband Joe Jonas, his brothers and bandmates Nick and Kevin, as well as their younger brother, Frankie, and Kevin's wife, Danielle. The move comes after Joe and Sophie's intense custody mediation and court appearances, which took place last week. The two reached a temporary agreement earlier this week that decided the former couple's two daughters, Willa and Delphine, will split their time between the U.S. with their dad and the U.K. with their mom.

The couple confirmed the development on Tuesday with a statement that read, "After a productive and successful mediation, we have agreed that the children will spend time equally in loving homes in both the U.S. and the U.K. We look forward to being great co-parents."

On Friday, Page Six reported that Joe filed to dismiss his Miami divorce case in order to reach a settlement outside of the court. In the documents obtained by the outlet, the two parties came to “various agreements” and decided to "pursue an amicable resolution of all issues.” The couple was married for four years.

Earlier this week, Joe also shared a cryptic photo to Instagram that captured his reflection in a mirror scrawled with the words "I am at the right place, at the right time, doing the right thing.”
