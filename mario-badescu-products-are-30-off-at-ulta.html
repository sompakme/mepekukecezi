Mario Badescu Products Are 30% Off at Ulta

Affordable skincare products and high-quality, effective beauty products might seem mutually exclusive, but Mario Badescu is proof you can, in fact, have the best of both worlds. The facialist-founded brand is delightfully fuss-free in every sense. Its pared-down packaging remains stylistically simple and the product labels sport straightforward verbiage. Its garnered countless loyal shoppers and some celebrity followers, including Ashley Graham and Olivia Culpo. And of course, the brand's price point is hard to beat.

Between now and December 10, you can score Mario Badescu products for 30 percent off when you shop the brand at Ulta. Many of the tried-and-true classics — like its drying lotion and rose water toner — are available during this limited-time offer. 

Whether you plan to stock up on your favorites or explore the brand for the first time, it’s the perfect opportunity to shop Mario Badescu while it’s still on sale and in stock. Below are our favorite picks to get you started, whether your shopping for holiday gifts or treating yourself.

Mini Mist Collection

Few face mists are as well-known as the Mario Badescu rosewater facial spray. If you’ve already tried the rose mist, why not branch out into its spinoffs, like aloe and cucumber, lavender, or orange blossom? All of these renditions and more are included in this adorable set of skin-quenching spritzes, making it the perfect holiday gift. One shopper wrote that the combination of sprays helps keep their skin looking "glowing and dewy" and "so hydrated."

Shop now: $8 (Originally $12); Ulta.com 

Glass Bottle Drying Lotion 

A must-have for thwarting burgeoning blemishes, this drying lotion is among the buzziest of the brand’s offerings. The formula contains sulfur and salicylic acid, both of which pummel pore-clogging debris and excess oil for skin that looks and feels clearer.

Shop now: $12 (Originally $17); Ulta.com

Enzyme Cleansing Gel 

Beloved by many of my beauty-industry peers who’ve lamented their shiny T-zones, this cleanser purifies pores of excess sebum, leaving skin with a matte finish. Fruit enzymes gently exfoliate for an instant boost of radiance post-use. Over time, skin is gloweir and more even-toned, too.

Shop now: $10 (Originally $14); Ulta.com

Glycolic Acid Toner 

Lauded for its exfoliating properties that leave skin glowing, glycolic acid is a superstar ingredient for imparting radiance in a flash. This toner contains a low percentage of the alpha hydroxy acid, making it gentle enough to use daily. Unlike many toners, this formula is alcohol-free, which lessens the risk for stripping skin or leaving it tight and dry.

Shop now: $13 (Originally $18); Ulta.com
