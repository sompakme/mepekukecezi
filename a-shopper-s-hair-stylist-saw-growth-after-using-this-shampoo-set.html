Though many effective hair-growth products — including serums, sprays, and supplements — exist, consistency is key in order to see results. Finally, argan oil softens and smooths hair partially due to its richness in vitamin E. Similarly, another reviewer says the set “made [their] hair grow” and “feel so much thicker.” Other reviewers swear the shampoo and conditioner leave hair significantly softer and shinier.
