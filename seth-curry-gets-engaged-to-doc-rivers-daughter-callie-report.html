Seth Curry Gets Engaged To Doc Rivers Daughter Callie: Report

July 29, 2024
blog

It looks like there’s some basketball royalty getting married. TMZ reports that Seth Curry, the younger brother of Steph, just proposed to his longtime girlfriend, Callie Rivers, who happens to be the daughter of Clippers coach Doc Rivers. And of course she said yes too.

The Blazers guard popped the question to his longtime girlfriend in front of their families at Ruth's Chris Steak House in Charlotte, North Carolina on Valentine's Day with brother Steph and father Dell present. Doc, Austin Rivers and the rest of the Rivers gang were also on hand for the special moment. Talk about a basketball dinner, the Curry’s & Rivers’ all at one table, my God.

Callie tweeted about the big day, saying "2.14.19 was a DAY. so thankful & blessed & loved & happy & positive & complete." However, at the time of the tweet we didn’t know she was engaged.

The Christmas & holiday basketball games are going to be epic at that family party. Check out her tweet (below) and congrats to the happy couple!
