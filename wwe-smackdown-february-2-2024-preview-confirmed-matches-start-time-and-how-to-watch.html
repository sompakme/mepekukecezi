WWE SmackDown February 2, 2024 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How to Watch

September 13, 2024
blog

The January 26th, 2024 episode of Friday Night SmackDown will be following the events of last week’s show, which had a lot of things going for it. It will also deal with the fallout of the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event, so fans can expect a lot of interesting segments as well.

Tonight’s episode of Friday Night SmackDown will be emanating from Legacy Arena in Birmingham, Alabama and will be airing live at 8 pm Eastern time slot on FOX. Naturally, there are numerous things fans can look forward to.

Having triumphed in the women’s Royal Rumble match, Bayley will decide whether to challenge Women’s World Champion Rhea Ripley or WWE Women’s Champion IYO SKY at WrestleMania 40 on SmackDown tonight.

Men’s Royal Rumble winner Cody Rhodes will also appear on the show. The WWE Undisputed Universal Champion Roman Reigns will also be present in the episode. Finally, United States Champion Logan Paul will address the crowd after his win at the Royal Rumble.

WWE Friday Night SmackDown Card for February 2nd, 2024 Episode:

  • Cody Rhodes appearance
  • Roman Reigns to continue his Road to WrestleMania 40 after the Royal Rumble
  • Bayley makes her decision regarding which Champion she’ll challenge at WrestleMania 40
  • Logan Paul’s first appearance since controversial Royal Rumble match
