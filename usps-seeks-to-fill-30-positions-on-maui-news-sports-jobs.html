USPS seeks to fill 30 positions on Maui | News, Sports, Jobs

August 17, 2024
blog

The U.S. Postal Service is seeking 30 new employees for seven Maui post offices and plans to hold a job fair from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. Thursday at the Makawao Post Office.

Open positions include rural carrier associates and assistant rural carriers. The jobs come with a starting pay of $23.82 an hour and would be located at the Haiku, Kihei, Kula, Lahaina, Makawao, Paia and Wailuku post offices.

Rural carrier associates case, deliver and collect mail along prescribed routes, according to a USPS news release. Assistant rural carriers deliver packages on Sundays and observed holidays. They may also case, deliver and collect mail and packages on Saturdays.

The Makawao Post Office is located at 1075 Makawao Ave.

Those unable to attend the job fair can apply online at usps.com/careers.
