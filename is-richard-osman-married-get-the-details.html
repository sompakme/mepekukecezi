    Is Richard Osman married? 

    Richard isn't married, but shares two children, Ruby and Sonny, with his first wife. The pair divorced back in 2007, and the star opened up to about ensuring that he spends time with the teenagers and to be different from his own father, who left the family home when Richard was ten. 

    Richard has two children from this first marriage 

    He said: "When my partnership broke up, I did think, 'Oh God, this is going to be awful.'... I've seen my kids nonstop. It was quickly worked out it wasn't going to be the same, which is an extraordinary relief. They know I love them and I tell them nonstop, which bores them rigid. But I never got told that."
