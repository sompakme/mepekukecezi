Pressing a red button gets The Voice judge Blake Shelton a massive salary Tue 23 November 2021

August 10, 2024
blog

The Voice judge Blake Shelton seems to have it all.

His marriage to music icon Gwen Stefani might have us in our feelings, but it’s his impressive salary from The Voice that has our jaws dropped.

Keep reading to find out the hefty amount Shelton earns from pressing that red button.

Blake Shelton judges on The Voice

The country singer has returned for Season 21 of The Voice, and we all want to be on Team Shelton.

The 45-year-old has coached eight champions over the course of the series, including Cam Anthony. That’s how you know he’s a real professional!

Stamped as a country music legend, there’s no wonder contestants on The Voice have chosen Blake to coach them. With 52 million singles and 13 million albums sold, the singer’s career is unmatched.

Blake Shelton’s impressive salary from The Voice revealed

Outside of his music career, Shelton has a dream job on The Voice – and the salary to show for it!

According to reports, Blake earns a hefty $13 million per season. If that isn’t enough for his pockets, The Voice used to air two seasons per year, so the country singer would likely bring in an impressive $26 million annually. What a big sum!

Moreover, Celebrity Insider reports that Shelton and Stefani were allegedly given an ‘exceptional bonus to keep the spark alive on-camera’, so who knows how much more the singer is raking in?

How do we apply for a job on The Voice?

The country singer has multiple streams of income

Blake Shelton earns millions from his role on The Voice, but he also has multiple other streams of income.

Obviously his successful music career packs in major earnings, but did you know that Shelton also opened a string of entertainment-themed restaurants and bars called Ole Red?

The first venue opened in 2017 in his hometown of Tishomingo, Oklahoma, though the restaurants are now dotted about nation-wide.

With all these streams of incomes combined, StyleCaster estimates Shelton’s overall net worth to be around $100M.
